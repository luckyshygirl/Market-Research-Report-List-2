<p><p>SMD (Surface Mount Device) power inductors are used in various electronic devices to store and release energy. There are three types of SMD power inductors: wire-wound type, multilayer type, and thin-film type. Wire-wound type inductors use a coiled wire to create a magnetic field, providing high inductance. Multilayer type inductors are made up of multiple layers of conductive and insulating materials, offering compact size and stable performance. Thin-film power inductors are constructed using thin metal film technology, offering precise inductance values and high current handling capabilities. These types cater to different power supply market requirements.</p></p>
