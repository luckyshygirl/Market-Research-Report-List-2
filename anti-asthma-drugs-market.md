<p><p>The Anti Asthma Drugs market offers various types of medications to manage and treat asthma symptoms. Bronchodilators help relax the muscles around the airways to improve breathing. Leukotriene antagonists work by blocking the actions of certain chemicals that cause inflammation and constriction of the airways.
