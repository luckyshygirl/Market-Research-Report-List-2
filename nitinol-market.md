<p><p>Nitinol, a type of shape memory alloy, is used in various industries due to its unique properties. The market for Nitinol can be classified into three types based on its memory effect. The 1-way memory effect allows for one predetermined shape, while the 2-way memory effect enables the material to revert between two predetermined shapes. However, the full memory effect is the most versatile, as it allows Nitinol to remember and transition between multiple shapes. These different market types cater to diverse applications and customer requirements in industries such as aerospace, medical, and robotics.</p></p>
