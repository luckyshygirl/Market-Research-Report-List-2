<p><p>Cast iron butterfly valves are a type of valve used for regulating or isolating the flow of various liquids and gases. These valves consist of a circular disc or plate, which is mounted on a rod in the center. The disc can be easily rotated to control the flow of the substance passing through the valve. Cast iron is chosen as the material for manufacturing these valves due to its durability and resistance to high temperatures.</p><p>The cast iron butterfly valves market is currently experiencing steady growth and is expected to continue to do so in the future.
