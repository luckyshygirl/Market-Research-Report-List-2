<p><p>The tunable ultrafast source market comprises two main types: femtosecond laser sources and picosecond laser sources. Femtosecond laser sources emit ultrafast pulses of light with durations in the femtosecond range (10^-15 seconds). These sources find applications in areas such as scientific research, medical imaging, and precision manufacturing. Picosecond laser sources emit pulses with durations in the picosecond range (10^-12 seconds). They are commonly used in fields like material processing, optical telecommunication, and spectroscopy. Both types of sources offer precise control over pulse duration and wavelength, catering to a wide range of industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
