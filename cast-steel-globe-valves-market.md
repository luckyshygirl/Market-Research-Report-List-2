<p><ul><li>Raised-Flange End</li><li>Ring-Type Joint End</li><li>Butt-Weld End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cast Steel Globe Valves market offers three types of valve ends: Raised-Flange End, Ring-Type Joint End, and Butt-Weld End. In the Raised-Flange End type, the valve is connected to the piping system using raised flange connections. The Ring-Type Joint End type involves the use of a special type of flange joint with a ring gasket for secure sealing. Lastly, the Butt-Weld End type involves welding the valve directly to the piping system, resulting in a strong and leak-proof connection. Each type offers different advantages based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
