<p><p>The Bluetooth headphones market can be categorized into three types. Mono Bluetooth headphones are designed to deliver sound through a single earpiece, ideal for phone calls and listening to audio in one ear. Ordinary stereo Bluetooth headphones offer sound through two earpieces, providing a traditional stereo sound experience for music, movies, and calls. True wireless stereo Bluetooth headphones are completely wireless, with separate earpieces for each ear, offering a more immersive and hassle-free listening experience without any wires. These different types cater to various user preferences and needs.</p></p>
