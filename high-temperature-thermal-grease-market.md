Conductivity Thermal Grease is designed to efficiently conduct heat, allowing for better heat dissipation and cooling. Non-conductive Thermal Grease, on the other hand, is used in situations where electrical conductivity needs to be avoided, preventing any potential damage or short circuits. Both types serve specific purposes in the management of heat in electronic devices.</p></p>
