<p><p>The sectional valve market consists of two main types: sectional directional valves and proportional directional valves. Sectional directional valves are used to control the direction and flow of hydraulic fluid in various applications. They allow for precise control and flexibility in hydraulic systems. Proportional directional valves, on the other hand, provide proportional control of the fluid flow based on the input signal received. These valves are commonly used in applications where precise control of speed and direction is required, such as in industrial machinery and automation systems.</p></p>
