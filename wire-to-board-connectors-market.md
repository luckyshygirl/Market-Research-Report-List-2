<p><p>Wire-to-board connectors are used to make electrical connections between wire harnesses and printed circuit boards (PCBs). The market for these connectors can be classified into three types based on pitch size: below 1.00 mm, 1.00 mm~2.00 mm, and above 2.00 mm. Connectors below 1.00 mm are thinner and provide high-density interconnections, making them suitable for compact electronic devices like smartphones. The 1.00 mm~2.00 mm connectors offer a balance between size and functionality, catering to a wide range of applications. Connectors above 2.00 mm are larger and are commonly used in industrial applications that require higher current carrying capacity.</p></p>
