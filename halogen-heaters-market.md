<p><p>Halogen heaters are a type of heating device that use halogen bulbs to produce heat. There are different types of halogen heaters available in the market. Oscillating heaters are designed to rotate horizontally, allowing heat to be distributed evenly throughout a room. This makes them suitable for larger spaces. On the other hand, blower type heaters are equipped with a fan that blows air over the halogen bulbs, resulting in quick and efficient heating. These heaters are ideal for small to medium-sized areas as they provide focused heat.</p></p>
