<p><p>The CNC Drilling market consists of two main types: Automatic and Semi-automatic. In the automatic market, drilling is fully controlled by computer programs without human intervention, providing high precision and efficiency. On the other hand, the semi-automatic market incorporates a combination of computer control and human supervision, allowing operators to make adjustments according to specific requirements and ensuring flexibility. These two types cater to different industry needs, with the automatic option being suitable for large-scale mass production, while the semi-automatic option is more ideal for smaller-scale production or customized drilling tasks.</p></p>
