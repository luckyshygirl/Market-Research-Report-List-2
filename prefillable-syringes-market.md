<p><p>Prefillable syringes refer to medical devices used for storing and administering medications. There are two types of prefillable syringes: plastic injectors and glass injectors. Plastic injectors are made from polypropylene and offer advantages such as enhanced safety features, ease of use, and cost-effectiveness.
