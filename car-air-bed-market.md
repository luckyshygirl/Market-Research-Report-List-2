On the other hand, TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) air beds are gaining popularity due to their eco-friendly nature and high elasticity. They provide a comfortable sleeping experience while being safe for the environment. Both PVC and TPE air beds cater to different consumer preferences and offer quality options for sleep or relaxation in a car.</p></p>
