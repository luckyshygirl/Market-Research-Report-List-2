<p><p>Tactile switches are categorized into different market types based on their specific features and usage. Standard types are the most commonly used switches, offering a basic and reliable tactile feedback when pressed. Illuminated types have additional LED lights embedded, providing visual feedback when activated. Sealed types are designed to be waterproof and resistant to external elements. SMD (Surface Mount Device) types are smaller in size and allow for easier installation on circuit boards. "Others" encompass specialized tactile switches that offer unique features or cater to specific applications.</p></p>
