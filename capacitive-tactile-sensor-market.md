<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitive Tactile Sensor</li><li>Projected Capacitive Tactile Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive tactile sensor market consists of two main types: surface capacitive tactile sensors and projected capacitive tactile sensors. Surface capacitive tactile sensors are placed on top of a surface and detect touch through changes in capacitance when a conductive object comes in contact with them. Projected capacitive tactile sensors are embedded beneath a surface and utilize a grid of electrodes to detect touch. Both sensors are widely used in various industries and applications requiring precise and responsive touch sensing capabilities.</p></p>
