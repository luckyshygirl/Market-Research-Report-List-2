<p><p>The Steel Core Aluminum Stranded Wire market consists of three main types: ACSR, ACSR/AW, and ACSR/TW. ACSR (Aluminum Conductors Steel Reinforced) comprises a central steel core surrounded by aluminum strands. It is widely used due to its strength and conductivity. ACSR/AW (Aluminum Conductors Aluminum Clad Steel Reinforced) features an aluminum-clad steel core, offering improved corrosion resistance. Lastly, ACSR/TW (Aluminum Conductors Steel Reinforced Tern) has triple steel strands to enhance its strength and durability. These wire types find applications in various industries requiring efficient and reliable transmission of electrical power.</p></p>
