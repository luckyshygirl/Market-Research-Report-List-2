<p><p>Frameless fans are a type of cooling fan that do not have a traditional housing or frame. They are essentially a motor and impeller assembly that circulates air without any enclosures. Within the frameless fans market, there are two main types: DC frameless fans and AC frameless fans. DC frameless fans are powered by direct current, which makes them energy-efficient and often used in low-voltage applications such as electronics cooling. AC frameless fans, on the other hand, are powered by alternating current and are commonly utilized in high-voltage applications like industrial cooling.</p></p>
