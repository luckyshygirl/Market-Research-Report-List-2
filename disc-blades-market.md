<p><p>The disc blades market consists of two main types: flat disc blades and concave disc blades. Flat disc blades have a flat surface and are commonly used for shallow tillage and seedbed preparation. They are effective in cutting, mixing, and turning soil. Concave disc blades, on the other hand, have a curved shape and are suitable for deep tillage and cutting through tough soil conditions. They are designed to penetrate the ground with less soil disturbance and offer improved soil penetration and residue cutting capabilities. Both types cater to different farming needs and are vital tools in agricultural practices.</p></p>
