<p><p>The Ammonium Sensor market can be categorized into two types: digital and analog. Digital sensors use numerical values to measure ammonium levels, providing more precise and accurate data. They use microprocessors and complex algorithms to convert analog signals into digital signals. On the other hand, analog sensors utilize continuous voltage signals to indicate ammonium concentration. They offer a simple and cost-effective solution for basic monitoring purposes. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used depending on the specific requirements of industries and applications.</p></p>
