<p><p>Shell and tube heat exchangers market is categorized into four types - L Type, M Type, N Type, and E Type. The L Type market refers to the heat exchangers with a long shell and a limited number of tubes, suitable for low-pressure applications. M Type heat exchangers have a medium shell length and tube count, making them versatile for various pressure ranges. N Type heat exchangers have a narrow shell and a high number of tubes, suitable for high-pressure applications. Finally, E Type heat exchangers have an extended surface area, allowing for efficient heat transfer in compact spaces.</p></p>
