<p><p>Mining metal detectors are widely used in the mining industry to detect and separate metal objects during the extraction process. The market for these detectors can be categorized into three types: flat type, frame type, and other markets. </p><p>- Flat type detectors consist of a flat surface that is placed on the conveyor belt. They are designed to detect metal objects that may be embedded in the material being transported. </p><p>- Frame type detectors, on the other hand, are installed around the conveyor belt and use a frame structure to detect metal contaminants in the material. </p><p>- The other markets category includes specialized metal detectors that cater to specific mining processes or applications, such as detecting metal in dense materials or underground mining operations. These detectors offer unique functionalities and customization options based on the specific requirements of the mining operation.</p></p>
