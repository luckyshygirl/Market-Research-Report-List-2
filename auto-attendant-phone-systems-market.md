<p><p>Auto attendant phone systems can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. </p><p>Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, as the hardware infrastructure is managed by the service provider. On-premises systems, on the other hand, are installed and maintained locally within an organization's premises. They offer greater control and security but require higher upfront investment and regular maintenance. Both market types aim to provide efficient call routing, voicemail, and call forwarding services for businesses.</p></p>
