<p><p>The High Viscosity Reactor market is segmented into different types, including Anchor Reactor, Frame Reactor, and Paddle Reactor. </p><p>An Anchor Reactor is designed with a central rotating agitator that moves in a circular motion, allowing efficient mixing and blending of high-viscosity materials. </p><p>A Frame Reactor consists of a rectangular frame that holds the reaction vessel, equipped with a rotating shaft and blades that provide mixing by creating turbulence. </p><p>Paddle Reactors have multiple paddles mounted on a central rotating shaft, allowing the solid and liquid components to be mixed effectively by creating a swirling motion.</p></p>
