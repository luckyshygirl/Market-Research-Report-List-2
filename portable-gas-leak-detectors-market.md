<p><p>The portable gas leak detectors market consists of different types of detectors, namely electrochemical, infrared, semiconductor, catalytic, and others. Electrochemical detectors work by utilizing a chemical reaction with gas to generate an electric current, providing accurate and sensitive results. Infrared detectors use infrared light absorption to detect gas leaks, making them ideal for detecting hydrocarbon gases. Semiconductor detectors employ a semiconductor material that reacts with gases, resulting in a change in electrical conductivity. Catalytic detectors use a catalytic bead to detect the presence of combustible gases. Other types of detectors may include ultrasonic or ultraviolet-based technologies.</p></p>
