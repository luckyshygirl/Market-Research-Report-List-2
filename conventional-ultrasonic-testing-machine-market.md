<p><strong>The Conventional Ultrasonic Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Transmission</li><li>Pulse Echo</li><li>Phased Array Pulse Echo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Conventional Ultrasonic Testing Machine market includes different types of machines used for testing materials. Through Transmission machines transmit ultrasound waves from one side and receive them on the other side to detect defects. Pulse Echo machines transmit ultrasound waves and measure the time taken for them to return, determining the thickness of the material and detecting defects. Phased Array Pulse Echo machines use multiple transducers to steer and focus ultrasound waves, allowing for detailed scanning and detecting defects in different angles. These machines are essential in ensuring the quality and integrity of materials in various industries.</p></p>
