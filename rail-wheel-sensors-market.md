<p><p>Rail wheel sensors are used in railway systems to monitor the movement and position of the train wheels. Single wheel sensors are designed to detect specific information from a single wheel, such as speed and revolution count. On the other hand, double wheel sensors are equipped to monitor two wheels simultaneously, providing more comprehensive data about wheel behavior, including wheel diameter and distance between wheels. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of railway systems.</p></p>
