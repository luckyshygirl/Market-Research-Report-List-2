<p><p>Smart stacker market includes different types of stackers such as trackless stacker and tracked stacker. Trackless stackers are designed with a compact footprint, making them ideal for use in narrow aisle applications where space is limited. They utilize advanced maneuvering technology, allowing them to navigate through tight spaces efficiently. On the other hand, tracked stackers are equipped with tracks for enhanced stability and traction, making them suitable for rough terrains or outdoor environments. Both types of stackers offer innovative solutions for material handling, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
