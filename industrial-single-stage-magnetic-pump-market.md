These pumps are widely used in industries such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and oil and gas.</p><p>The Industrial Single-stage Magnetic Pump Market is experiencing steady growth and is expected to grow at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 5.7% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Industrial Single-stage Magnetic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial single-stage magnetic pump market consists of two types: stationary shaft and rotating shaft. In the stationary shaft market, the pump impeller is connected to a stationary shaft, which eliminates the need for mechanical seals and results in a leak-free design. This type is suitable for applications with corrosive or hazardous fluids. On the other hand, the rotating shaft market involves a pump impeller connected to a rotating shaft, allowing the pump to handle high pressures and flow rates efficiently. Both types offer reliable and efficient pumping solutions for various industrial applications.</p></p>
