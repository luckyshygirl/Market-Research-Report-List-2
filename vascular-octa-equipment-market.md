There are different types of OCTA equipment available in the market. SD-OCT (Spectral Domain OCT) utilizes a broadband light source to generate high-resolution images of blood flow. Swept-Source OCT combines a tunable laser source with a detector for efficient image acquisition. Other variations of OCTA equipment may include specialized features and capabilities tailored to specific clinical needs. These equipment help healthcare professionals visualize and analyze blood vessel structures, aiding in the diagnosis and monitoring of various vascular disorders.</p></p>
