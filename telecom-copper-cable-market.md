<p><p>The Telecom Copper Cable Market consists of two main types: Dry Core and Jelly-filled Core. Dry Core cables are designed without any external filling material, where the conductors are insulated by air. This type of cable is commonly used for indoor applications. On the other hand, Jelly-filled Core cables have a filling compound around the conductors to protect them from moisture and corrosion. This type of cable is primarily used for outdoor or underground installations where exposure to moisture is a concern. Both types have their specific applications and advantages based on the environment they are used in.</p></p>
