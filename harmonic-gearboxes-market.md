<p><p>Harmonic gearboxes are used in various industries for precision motion control applications. There are three main types: cup style, hat style, and pancake style. Cup style harmonic gearboxes have a compact design and offer high torque capacity. Hat style gearboxes feature a flanged design, providing better torsional rigidity and torque transmission. Pancake style gearboxes are characterized by their flat configuration and exceptional backlash reduction. Each type caters to specific needs, enabling efficient power transmission and ensuring smooth and precise movements in different applications.</p></p>
