<p><p>The radiology microcatheter market consists of different types, namely diagnostic, delivery, steerable, and aspiration microcatheters. Diagnostic microcatheters are used to inject contrast media into blood vessels, helping visualize and diagnose a wide range of conditions. Delivery microcatheters aid in the precise delivery of therapeutic devices, such as stents or coils. Steerable microcatheters offer increased maneuverability, allowing physicians to navigate complex blood vessels and reach targeted areas. Aspiration microcatheters are designed to remove unwanted materials, including blood clots or emboli, from blood vessels.</p></p>
