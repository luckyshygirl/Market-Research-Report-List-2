<p><p>BOPA laminating film is used to enhance the packaging quality of various products. It is manufactured through two market types: flat-film extrusion and tube film extrusion. In flat-film extrusion, the film is produced in a flat form, allowing for customization and suitability for different applications. On the other hand, tube film extrusion involves manufacturing the film in a tube-like shape, which offers benefits such as air and moisture resistance. Both techniques play a crucial role in the BOPA laminating film market, catering to diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
