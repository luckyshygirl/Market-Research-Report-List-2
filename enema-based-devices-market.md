<p><p>The enema based devices market can be categorized into several types. Cleansing enemas are used to cleanse the colon and remove waste materials. Carminative enemas help relieve gas and bloating in the digestive system. Retention enemas involve retaining the enema solution inside the rectum for a specific period to achieve therapeutic effects. Return-flow enemas involve alternating the flow of solution into and out of the rectum, aiding in bowel movement. Lastly, other types of enemas include nutrient enemas for providing nutrition and medicated enemas for delivering specific medications. These devices cater to different purposes of maintaining a healthy digestive system.</p></p>
