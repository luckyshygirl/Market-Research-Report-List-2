<p><strong>The Tubular Centrifuges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GF Type</li><li>GQ Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tubular centrifuges are classified into two main market types, namely GF type and GQ type. The GF type is designed for solid-liquid separation and is widely used in industries such as chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing. It efficiently separates solids from liquids by spinning at a high speed, causing the heavier particles to settle at the bottom. On the other hand, the GQ type is specifically designed for liquid-liquid separation and is commonly used in applications requiring high-purity products. It uses the principle of density difference to separate immiscible liquids. Both market types play crucial roles in various industries that require effective separation processes.</p></p>
