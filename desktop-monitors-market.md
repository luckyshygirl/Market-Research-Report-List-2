On the other hand, Curved Type Monitors have a gently curved shape, which offers an immersive viewing experience by reducing distortion and creating a wider field of view. These monitors are commonly preferred for tasks that require enhanced visual depth, such as content creation, design, and immersive gaming.</p></p>
