<p><p>The commercial brush cutter market can be categorized into two major types: corded and cordless brush cutters. Corded brush cutters are powered by electricity and require a constant power source. They are suitable for small to medium-sized areas where the power cord can reach. On the other hand, cordless brush cutters operate on rechargeable batteries, offering more flexibility and mobility. They are ideal for larger areas or places without access to electricity. Both corded and cordless brush cutters are designed for commercial use, providing efficient cutting and clearance of dense vegetation.</p></p>
