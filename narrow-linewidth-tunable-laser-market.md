<p><p>The Narrow Linewidth Tunable Laser market can be categorized into different types. Semiconductor Lasers are compact and versatile devices that emit narrow linewidth light. Distributed Feedback Laser Diodes (DFB Lasers) are semiconductor lasers that use a periodic grating structure to produce a stable single-mode output. Distributed Bragg Reflector Lasers (DBR Lasers) also use a grating structure for wavelength selection. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various types of lasers used for tunable applications, such as External Cavity Lasers (ECL) and Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Lasers (VCSELs).</p></p>
