<p><p>A range cooker, also known as a freestanding cooker or a stove, is a versatile kitchen appliance that combines a cooktop and an oven in a single unit. Range cookers offer a wide range of cooking options, with their multiple burners and ovens allowing for simultaneous cooking of different dishes at different temperatures, making them ideal for large families or those who love entertaining guests.</p><p>Furthermore, the advanced features and technological innovations, such as programmable timers, touch controls, smart connectivity, self-cleaning functions, and energy efficiency, have attracted consumer attention and contributed to the market growth.</p><p>Looking ahead, the range cooker market is expected to continue its upward trajectory.
