<p><p>The orthopedic trauma fixation product market is divided into different types of products. Internal fixation groups include plates, screws, nails, and other devices that are surgically implanted inside the body to stabilize fractured bones. These products help in the healing process by providing internal support. On the other hand, external fixation refers to devices that are attached outside the body to hold broken bones in proper alignment. These external fixators are commonly used in cases where internal fixation is not suitable or possible. The market for these products caters to the needs of patients with orthopedic trauma.</p></p>
