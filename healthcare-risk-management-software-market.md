<p><p>Healthcare risk management software market can be classified into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and managed locally by the healthcare provider on their own server. It requires extensive hardware and IT resources, but provides greater control and security over data. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. Data is securely stored in the cloud, allowing easy remote access and collaboration among healthcare professionals.</p></p>
