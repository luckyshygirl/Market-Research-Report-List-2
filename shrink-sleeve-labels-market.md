<p><p>The shrink sleeve labels market consists of various types of materials, including PVC, PET-G, expanded polystyrene films, PE, polylactic acid films, and others. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is a widely used material known for its durability and flexibility. PET-G (polyethylene terephthalate glycol) offers excellent clarity and is often used for products requiring high visibility. Expanded polystyrene films provide cushioning and protection. PE (polyethylene) is a versatile material that can be easily recycled. Polylactic acid films are made from renewable resources, making them environmentally friendly. Other materials used in shrink sleeve labels include polyester, OPS (oriented polystyrene), and PLA (polylactic acid).</p></p>
