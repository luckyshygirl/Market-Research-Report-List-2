<p><p>Gas turbine flow meters are used to measure the flow rate of gases like natural gas, steam, and compressed air. There are several types of gas turbine flow meters available in the market. </p><p>One type is the axial type, which uses a straight-through flow path and measures the flow velocity in the axial direction. </p><p>Another type is the tangential type, which utilizes a tangential flow path to measure the rotational velocity of the gas. </p><p>Mechanical type gas turbine flow meters have moving parts like blades or rotors that rotate with the flow, generating a rotational speed measurement. </p><p>Lastly, there are other types of gas turbine flow meters that use different principles or technologies to measure the flow rate, such as ultrasonic or thermal-based methods.</p></p>
