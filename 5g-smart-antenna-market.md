<p><ul><li>Switched Multi-beam Antenna</li><li>Adaptive Array Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Switched Multi-beam Antenna is a type of 5G smart antenna that uses multiple beams to transmit and receive signals. It has the ability to dynamically switch between different beams, allowing for simultaneous communication with multiple devices. This technology improves network capacity and enhances the efficiency of data transmission in congested areas. On the other hand, Adaptive Array Antenna uses multiple antenna elements to dynamically adapt the radiation pattern to optimize signal quality and coverage. It can adjust the beam direction and shape based on the user's location, resulting in improved signal strength and reduced interference.</p></p>
