<p><p>The Stationary Sprinkler Irrigation System market includes various types of irrigation methods. Center Pivot irrigation involves a circular system where water sprays from a pivot point, covering a large area. Lateral Move irrigation employs a linear system where sprinklers move slowly across the field. Solid Set irrigation refers to a fixed system with permanently installed sprinklers. Other types include drip irrigation, which delivers water directly to the plants, and hydroponic irrigation, which involves growing plants in water. These methods are utilized in agriculture and landscaping to efficiently distribute water for crop growth.</p></p>
