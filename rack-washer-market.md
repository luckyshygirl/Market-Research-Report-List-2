<p><p>The rack washer market consists of two main types: Single Door Pass-through and Double Door Pass-throughs. Single Door Pass-throughs are rack washers with a single door for loading and unloading racks. They are typically used in smaller facilities where space is limited. Double Door Pass-throughs, on the other hand, have two doors, allowing racks to be loaded and unloaded from opposite ends. These types are commonly found in larger operations where multiple racks need to be cleaned simultaneously.</p></p>
