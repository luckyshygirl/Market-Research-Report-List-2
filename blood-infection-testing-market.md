<p><p>Blood infection testing, also known as blood culture testing, is a diagnostic procedure used to detect the presence of bacteria, fungi, or viruses in the bloodstream. The sample is then analyzed to identify the specific microorganism causing the infection, which helps determine the appropriate course of treatment.</p><p>The global blood infection testing market is projected to exhibit significant growth in the coming years.
