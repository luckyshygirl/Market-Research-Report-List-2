<p><p>HVAC terminal units refer to the devices used to control the temperature and airflow in a heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system. Single duct terminal units supply only conditioned air to the space, while dual duct units supply both hot and cold air. Fan powered terminal units incorporate a fan to boost the airflow. These different types serve specific needs in HVAC systems, with single duct being simple and cost-effective, dual duct providing more precise temperature control, and fan powered offering increased airflow for larger spaces or to overcome pressure variations.</p></p>
