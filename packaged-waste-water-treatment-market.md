<p><strong>The Packaged Waste Water Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Packaged Waste Water Treatment market includes various types of technologies such as MBR, MBBR, SBR, and Extended Aeration. MBR (Membrane BioReactor) employs a membrane to treat the water and remove contaminants. MBBR (Moving Bed BioReactor) uses a biofilm that grows on plastic carriers to break down pollutants. SBR (Sequential Batch Reactor) treats wastewater in cycles, with each cycle including different stages of treatment. Extended Aeration involves the use of activated sludge to break down organic matter. These technologies offer efficient and compact solutions for wastewater treatment in various applications.</p></p>
