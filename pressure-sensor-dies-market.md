<p><strong>The Pressure Sensor Dies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive</li><li>Capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure sensor dies are used to measure and monitor pressure levels in various industrial applications. There are different types of pressure sensor dies available in the market, namely piezoresistive and capacitive sensors. </p><p>Piezoresistive pressure sensor dies operate by utilizing the change in electrical resistance of materials under pressure, allowing for accurate pressure measurements. Capacitive pressure sensor dies, on the other hand, rely on changes in capacitance between two conductive plates to determine pressure levels. Both types offer reliable and precise measurement capabilities, catering to different industry needs.</p></p>
