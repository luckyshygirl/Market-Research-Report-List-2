<p><p>Minimally invasive surgical instruments are specialized tools used in minimally invasive surgeries, which involve small incisions rather than large openings. They are designed to access specific body parts, perform delicate procedures, and provide enhanced visualizations through endoscopic cameras.</p><p>The global minimally invasive surgical instruments market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. The market is driven by factors such as the increasing number of surgical procedures, rising prevalence of chronic diseases, advancements in technology, and growing adoption of minimally invasive techniques by healthcare providers. Additionally, favorable reimbursement policies and rising healthcare expenditure are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its dynamics. One such trend is the development of robotic-assisted surgical instruments. These instruments provide enhanced precision, control, and navigational capabilities, thereby improving surgical outcomes.
