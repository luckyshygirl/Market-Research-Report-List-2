<p><p>The plastic films and sheets market involves various types of films and sheets used for packaging, construction, and other applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) and LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) are flexible and widely used for packaging. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is known for its durability and is commonly used for pipes and cables. PA (Polyamide) films are strong and used for food packaging. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) films have good clarity and are used for labeling and packaging. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) films are strong and resistant to chemicals. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) films are used for packaging and lamination. PES (Polyester) films have excellent tensile strength and are used in electrical insulation. Other types of films and sheets include polycarbonate, acrylic, and polypropylene films.</p></p>
