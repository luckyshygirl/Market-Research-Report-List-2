<p><p>Cutting tools are instruments used for removing material from a workpiece by means of shearing, abrasion, or any other cutting process. They include various tools and equipment such as toolholders, cutting inserts, drill chucks, rotary tables, and workpiece clamping devices.</p><p>The cutting tool and machine tool accessory market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Several factors are driving this market growth, including the increasing adoption of CNC machines in various industries, the growing automotive and aerospace sectors, and the rise in demand for high-quality and precision engineering tools.
