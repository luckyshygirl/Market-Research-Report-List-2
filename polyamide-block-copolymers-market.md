<p><p>Polyamide block copolymers are widely used in various markets, including Injection Molding, Blow Molding, Thermoforming, and Extrusion Molding. In Injection Molding, the copolymers are melted and injected into a mold, creating complex shapes and designs. Blow Molding involves melting the copolymers and blowing them into a mold to create hollow, thin-walled products. Thermoforming utilizes heat to shape the copolymers into desired forms, while Extrusion Molding involves melting the copolymers and forcing them through a die to create continuous profiles or sheets. These processes offer versatile applications in various industries.</p></p>
