<p><p>Cast iron is a strong, brittle iron alloy that is commonly used in manufacturing. The cast iron castings market is divided into two types of molding processes - vertical and horizontal moldings. Vertical molding involves pouring molten cast iron into a vertically oriented mold, which offers advantages like high repeatability and good surface finish. On the other hand, horizontal molding involves pouring molten cast iron into a horizontally oriented mold, which is suitable for producing larger castings and offers advantages like better casting density. These different molding processes cater to diverse manufacturing needs in the cast iron industry.</p></p>
