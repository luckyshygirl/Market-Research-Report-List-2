<p><strong>The Non contact Infrared Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li><li>SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-contact infrared temperature sensor market is categorized into two types: through-hole thermopile infrared sensor and SMD thermopile infrared sensor. Through-hole thermopile infrared sensors are designed to be mounted on a printed circuit board using holes. They provide accurate temperature measurement and are widely used in industrial applications. On the other hand, SMD thermopile infrared sensors are surface-mount devices that can be directly soldered onto the PCB. They offer smaller size, higher sensitivity, and cost-effective solution for temperature sensing in various industries.</p></p>
