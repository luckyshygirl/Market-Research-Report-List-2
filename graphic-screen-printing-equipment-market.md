<p><p>Graphic screen printing equipment refers to machinery used for printing graphics on various products such as textiles, paper, and plastic. These machines can be categorized into three types based on their level of automation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual equipment requires operators to manually load and unload products onto the printing surface, while semi-automatic equipment automates some processes such as ink application or curing. Fully automatic equipment performs all tasks without human intervention, including product loading, ink application, and curing. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of productivity, cost, and flexibility in graphic screen printing operations.</p></p>
