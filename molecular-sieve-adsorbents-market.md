<p><p>Molecular sieve adsorbents are materials used for separating and purifying gases and liquids by selectively adsorbing specific molecules. There are various types of molecular sieve adsorbents available in the market. </p><p>3A, 4A, 5A are named based on their pore size and selectivity for certain molecules. Type 3A is suitable for adsorbing water and ethanol, while 4A is effective for removing water, carbon dioxide, and mercaptans. 5A is used for selectively adsorbing various molecules like carbon monoxide, ethylene, and propylene.</p><p>Type X molecular sieve adsorbents have large pore sizes suitable for separating nitrogen and oxygen from air. Other markets in this sector include hybrid adsorbents, zeolite-based adsorbents, and specialty adsorbents tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
