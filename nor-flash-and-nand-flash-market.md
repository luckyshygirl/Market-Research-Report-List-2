<p><p>NOR Flash and NAND Flash are two types of non-volatile storage technologies widely used in the semiconductor industry. It is often used for firmware storage in devices such as microcontrollers, automotive systems, and consumer electronics. On the other hand, NAND Flash is a type of memory that allows only sequential access, making it more suitable for mass storage applications such as solid-state drives (SSDs), USB drives, and memory cards.</p><p>The current outlook of the NOR Flash and NAND Flash market is highly promising.
