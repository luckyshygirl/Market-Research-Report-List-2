<p><p>Flow chemistry reactors refer to devices used for carrying out chemical reactions in continuous flow conditions. There are several types of flow chemistry reactors available in the market. Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR) are characterized by a constant mixing of reactants, while Plug Flow Reactors (PFR) enable reactants to flow through a fixed tubular structure. Micro Reactor Systems (MRT) are compact devices used for performing reactions on a small scale. The "Others" category includes additional types of reactors that do not fall into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
