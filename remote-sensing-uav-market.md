<p><p>The remote sensing UAV market consists of two main types: fixed-wing UAVs and helicopter UAVs. Fixed-wing UAVs are unmanned aerial vehicles that have elongated wings and rely on forward motion to generate lift. They are known for their long flight endurance and large coverage area, making them suitable for large-scale mapping and surveillance applications. In contrast, helicopter UAVs have rotary wings and are capable of vertical takeoff and landing. They offer greater agility and maneuverability, making them ideal for close-range missions that require precise and flexible maneuvering.</p></p>
