<p><p>Truffle oil is classified into two main market types: black truffle oil and white truffle oil. Black truffle oil is made from black truffle extracts and has a strong, earthy flavor. It is often used to enhance the taste of savory dishes like pasta, risotto, or grilled meats. On the other hand, white truffle oil is derived from white truffle essence and possesses a more delicate and subtle flavor. It is commonly used as a finishing touch or drizzle on dishes like salads, scrambled eggs, or creamy soups. Both types cater to different taste preferences and can elevate various culinary creations.</p></p>
