<p><p>Protective pipe coatings refer to the application of various substances onto the internal and external surfaces of pipes to provide protection against corrosion and other damages. Internal coating involves the application of coatings on the inner surface of pipes, preventing the deposition of minerals and chemicals and enhancing the flow of fluid. External coating, on the other hand, is applied to the outer surface of pipes to shield them from environmental factors such as weathering, UV radiation, and mechanical impacts. These coatings ensure the longevity and integrity of pipes, minimizing maintenance requirements.</p></p>
