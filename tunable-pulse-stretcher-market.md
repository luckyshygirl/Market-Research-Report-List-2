<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Tunable Pulse Stretcher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Bragg grating Dispersion(FBG)</li><li>Chirped Fiber Bragg Grating(CFBG)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tunable pulse stretcher market consists of two main types: Fiber Bragg grating Dispersion (FBG) and Chirped Fiber Bragg Grating (CFBG). FBG relies on a specific type of fiber grating to stretch and delay pulses of light, allowing for precise control over the pulse duration. CFBG, on the other hand, utilizes a fiber grating with a continuously varying period, resulting in a more gradual pulse elongation. Both technologies cater to different applications in fields such as telecommunications, optical sensing, and spectroscopy, providing versatile options for pulse shaping and manipulation.</p></p>
