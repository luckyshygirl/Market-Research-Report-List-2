<p><p>In-mold labeling machines refer to a type of labeling process where preprinted labels are inserted into the mold before the plastic product is formed. There are three main market types for in-mold labeling machines: injection molding, blow molding, and thermoforming. In the injection molding type, labels are inserted into the mold cavities before the injection of molten plastic. The blow molding type involves placing the label in the mold before the plastic parison is inflated. Lastly, in thermoforming, labels are inserted into the mold before plastic sheets are heated and formed.</p></p>
