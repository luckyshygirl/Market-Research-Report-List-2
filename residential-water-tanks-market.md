<p><p>Residential water tanks are storage containers used to collect and store rainwater or other sources of water for domestic use in residential buildings. These tanks are designed to be installed outside the house and connected to the existing water supply system or used for standalone purposes, providing an additional water source for various household applications such as gardening, toilet flushing, laundry, and cleaning.</p><p>The residential water tanks market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the rising awareness regarding sustainable water management practices and the need to conserve water resources is driving the demand for residential water tanks. Additionally, the increasing incidents of water scarcity and drought in various regions are further fueling the adoption of these tanks as a reliable backup water source.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing population and urbanization, particularly in developing countries, are also contributing to the market growth. With more people moving to cities and urban areas, the demand for water supply is increasing, leading to a greater need for efficient storage solutions. Residential water tanks offer a practical solution to meet the rising water demand in urban households.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing preference for aesthetically appealing and space-saving designs in residential water tanks. Manufacturers are focusing on developing slimline or underground tanks that can be easily integrated into modern urban landscapes without compromising on storage capacity.</p><p>Overall, the residential water tanks market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
