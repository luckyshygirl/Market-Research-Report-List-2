<p><p>Positive displacement needleless connectors are medical devices used to connect intravenous lines and catheters without the need for a needle. There are two types of connectors: simple and complex needleless connectors. Simple connectors have a basic design with a single internal seal, while complex connectors have multiple internal seals, valves, and additional features to prevent catheter-related bloodstream infections. Both types aim to improve patient safety by reducing the risk of contamination, catheter occlusions, and accidental needlesticks while providing a convenient and efficient method of connection for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
