<p><p>The Data Acquisition Unit (DAU) market has two main types: Portable and Desktop. Portable DAUs are compact and designed for on-the-go use. They are commonly used in field applications, such as environmental monitoring or mobile data collection. Portable DAUs offer flexible connectivity options and are easy to transport. On the other hand, Desktop DAUs are larger and meant for stationary use. They are prevalent in professional research labs or industrial settings where large amounts of measurements need to be recorded. Desktop DAUs generally have more input channels and higher data processing capabilities.</p></p>
