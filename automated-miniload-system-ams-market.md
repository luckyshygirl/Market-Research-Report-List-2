<p><p>Automated Miniload System (AMS) refers to a warehousing solution that utilizes automation technology for the handling and storage of small to medium-sized products. The AMS market can be categorized into three types: Conveyor System, Stacker Cranes, and Others. A Conveyor System involves the use of automated conveyor belts to transport products within the warehouse. Stacker Cranes, on the other hand, incorporate robotic cranes to move and stack products. The "Others" category refers to alternative automation technologies not included in the previous two types, such as robotic arms or shuttle systems.</p></p>
