<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Metal 3D Printer market consists of two types: Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electronic Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse layers of metal powder together, creating complex 3D objects. This process is suitable for various metals and allows for high precision and accuracy in manufacturing parts. On the other hand, EBM uses an electron beam to melt and fuse metal powders. It is particularly effective for materials with high melting points and is known for its faster build rates. Both technologies are gaining prominence in the metal 3D printing industry.</p></p>
