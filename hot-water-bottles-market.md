<p><p>Hot water bottles are available in two main market types: non-chargeable and chargeable. Non-chargeable hot water bottles are designed to be filled with hot water manually, typically using a kettle or hot tap water. They provide warmth and relief but need to be refilled when the water cools down. On the other hand, chargeable hot water bottles come with a built-in heating element that can be charged using an electric power source. These bottles offer convenience as they can provide heat for a longer duration without the need for refilling.</p></p>
