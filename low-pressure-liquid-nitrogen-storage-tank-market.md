<p><p>The low pressure liquid nitrogen storage tank market includes two main types: regular storage tanks and dry shippers. Regular storage tanks are designed to store liquid nitrogen under low pressure in a controlled environment, enabling easy access and long-term storage. On the other hand, dry shippers are specifically designed for transporting biological samples or valuable specimens. These tanks use a dry vapor chamber to maintain a stable temperature without the need for liquid nitrogen, ensuring the safety and integrity of the transported items.</p></p>
