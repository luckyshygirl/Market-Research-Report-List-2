Hydrophilic coatings improve the implants' lubricity and reduce friction. Super hydrophilic coatings offer an even higher level of lubrication, minimizing tissue damage. Drug-loaded coatings release therapeutic substances to promote healing and prevent infections. Anticoagulant coatings prevent blood clot formation on the implant's surface. Antimicrobial coatings inhibit the growth of bacteria and other microorganisms, reducing the risk of infection. Lastly, the "Other" category includes additional specialized coatings tailored for specific applications in implant technology.</p></p>
