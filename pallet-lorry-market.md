<p><p>A pallet lorry, also known as a pallet jack or pallet truck, is a material handling equipment used to lift and transport palletized goods within a warehouse or a distribution center. Pallet lorries are essential tools in the logistics industry, as they help in improving productivity and efficiency by reducing the physical strain on workers and streamlining the movement of goods.</p><p>The future outlook of the pallet lorry market looks promising. <p><p>The pallet lorry market is divided into two main types: manual pallet trucks and electric pallet trucks. Manual pallet trucks are operated by a person pushing or pulling the truck to transport pallets. They are generally less expensive and more suitable for smaller warehouses. On the other hand, electric pallet trucks are powered by batteries and require less physical effort to operate. They are ideal for larger warehouses or heavy pallet loads, as they offer increased efficiency and load handling capabilities. Both types of pallet trucks serve the purpose of transporting goods on pallets efficiently within a warehouse setting.</p></p>
