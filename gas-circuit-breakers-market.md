<p><p>Gas circuit breakers are categorized based on their voltage levels. These include medium voltage (MV), high voltage (HV), extra high voltage (EHV), and ultra-high voltage (UHV) markets. MV gas circuit breakers are designed for electrical systems with voltages ranging from 1kV to 52kV. HV gas circuit breakers are used in systems with voltages ranging from 52kV to 1100kV. EHV circuit breakers are employed in systems with voltages between 1100kV and 1700kV. Lastly, UHV gas circuit breakers are utilized in systems with voltages exceeding 1700kV.</p></p>
