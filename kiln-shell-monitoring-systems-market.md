Two types of systems are commonly used in the market: standard scanners and stereo scanners. Standard scanners provide a single-view image of the kiln shell, while stereo scanners capture multiple views, allowing for a more detailed analysis. Both types of systems utilize advanced technologies to detect and prevent potential issues such as shell deformations, hot spots, and brick fallouts, ensuring the safe and efficient operation of kilns in various industries.</p></p>
