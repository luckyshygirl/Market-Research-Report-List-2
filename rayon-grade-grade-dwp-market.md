<p><p>Rayon Grade DWP refers to the quality of wood pulp used in the production of rayon fibers. There are two main types of wood pulp used: alkali wood pulp and sulfate wood pulp. Alkali wood pulp is produced through a chemical process using alkaline substances, while sulfate wood pulp is made through a process involving sulfates. Additionally, there is wood pulp of sulfite, which is produced using a different method involving sulfite compounds. The choice of wood pulp type affects the quality and properties of the resulting rayon fibers.</p></p>
