<p><p>Nov-woven fabrics absorbent pads for food packaging come in two main types - staple fibre and long fibres. Staple fibre pads are made from short strands of synthetic or natural fibres, which are intertwined to create a fabric that is highly absorbent and effective in soaking up liquids. On the other hand, long fibre pads are composed of longer strands of fibres, either natural or synthetic, providing improved strength and durability. Both types of pads are widely used in the food packaging industry to prevent moisture and liquid leakage.</p></p>
