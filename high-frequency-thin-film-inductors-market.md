<p><strong>The High Frequency Thin Film Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Plug-in Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High frequency thin film inductors are categorized into two types - SMD type and plug-in type. SMD type inductors are surface mount components, meaning they can be directly mounted onto the surface of a circuit board, enabling faster and more efficient assembly. Plug-in type inductors, on the other hand, require manual insertion into circuit boards using pins or leads. Both types are used in high frequency applications, but the choice depends on design requirements, manufacturing processes, and circuit board configurations.</p></p>
