<p><p>The Submerged Arc Welded Pipe market consists of two main types: LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welding) Pipe and SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welding) Pipe. LSAW Pipe is manufactured by bending and welding steel plates longitudinally, creating a cylindrical shape. It is mainly used for the transportation of oil, gas, and water. On the other hand, SSAW Pipe is manufactured by spirally bending steel coils and welding them together. They are commonly used for pile foundations, oil and gas transportation, and structural applications. Both types play crucial roles in the construction and infrastructure industries.</p></p>
