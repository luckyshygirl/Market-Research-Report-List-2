<p><p>Straw crushing and briquetting machine is a versatile and efficient agricultural machine that is used to crush and compress straw into briquettes for various applications. The crushed straw is then fed into the briquette making machine, which compresses it under high pressure and temperature to form solid briquettes. <p><p>Straw crushing and briquetting machines are used to process straw into biomass fuel. There are three types of machines available in the market: mechanical, hydraulic, and screw extrusion. Mechanical machines operate by using a rotating mechanism to crush and compact the straw. Hydraulic machines use hydraulic pressure to compress the straw into briquettes. Screw extrusion machines combine the crushing and briquetting process into one, utilizing a screw mechanism to crush and extrude the straw material. Each type has its advantages and suitability for different applications in the market.</p></p>
