<p><p>Crystal diodes are electronic components that allow electric current to flow through them in one direction. There are different types of crystal diodes available in the market, including Germanium, Silicon, and Others. Germanium diodes are made from germanium material and offer lower forward voltage drop, which is beneficial for certain applications. Silicon diodes are made from silicon material, offering better temperature stability and higher current capabilities. Other types of crystal diodes include Schottky diodes, Gunn diodes, and Varactor diodes, each having their unique properties suitable for specific electronic applications.</p></p>
