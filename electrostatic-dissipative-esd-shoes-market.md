<p><p>Electrostatic Dissipative (ESD) shoes, also known as anti-static shoes, are designed to control or minimize the buildup of static electricity in the body. They are commonly used in environments where static electricity can create a risk of damage to sensitive electronic components or cause harm to individuals.</p><p>ESD shoes typically feature conductive materials in their sole and a conductive grounding strap that is connected to the grounding system of the workplace. This allows static charges to be safely discharged to the ground, preventing damage to electronic devices or shock to the wearer.</p><p>According to a market analysis, the Electrostatic Dissipative (ESD) Shoes Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period. This trend is driven by the increasing demand for ESD shoes in industries where workers are required to wear them for long durations.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in ESD shoe technology, such as the integration of smart features like temperature and pressure sensors, are also observed in the market.
