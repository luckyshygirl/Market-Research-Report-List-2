Governments are imposing strict limits on VOC emissions and encouraging the use of low or zero VOC products. High solid solvent-type epoxy coatings have a reduced solvent content, minimizing VOC emissions. Powder coating type epoxy formulations are applied electrostatically and then cured through heat. Radiation curing type epoxy coatings use UV or electron beam radiation for a rapid solidification process.</p></p>
