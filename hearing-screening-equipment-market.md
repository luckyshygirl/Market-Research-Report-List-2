<p><p>Hearing screening equipment is used to test the hearing ability of individuals. Two main types of equipment are commonly used - OAE (Otoacoustic Emissions) and ABR (Auditory Brainstem Response) screening equipment. OAE equipment measures the sounds generated by the inner ear in response to a stimulus, whereas ABR equipment measures the electrical activity of the auditory nerve and brainstem in response to sound. Both types of equipment help in identifying hearing impairments and determining the appropriate course of treatment for individuals.</p></p>
