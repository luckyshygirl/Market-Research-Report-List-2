<p><p>Electroplating system market types refer to different methods of implementing electroplating processes. </p><p>Continuous plating involves a continuous operation, where objects move through a plating bath on a conveyor belt. This method ensures a constant flow of objects to be plated.</p><p>Line plating, on the other hand, involves a stationary setup, where objects are individually placed on a rack or a line that is immersed into the plating bath. This method allows for more control and precision in the plating process.</p><p>Other markets in the electroplating system may include specialized systems for particular industries or unique applications, such as barrel plating for small parts or selective plating for intricate designs.</p></p>
