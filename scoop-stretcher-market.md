<p><p>A scoop stretcher, also known as a clamshell stretcher or scoop litter, is a medical device used to safely transport injured patients, particularly those with potential spinal injuries. It consists of two separate halves connected by hinges at the midpoint, allowing them to be opened and closed like a clamshell. The stretcher can be placed under the patient and then closed, securely encasing them and providing rigid support during transfer.</p><p>The scoop stretcher market has been experiencing steady growth due to the increasing use of these devices in emergency medical services (EMS), hospitals, and other healthcare facilities. The rising number of accidents and trauma cases, coupled with the growing awareness about the importance of proper patient handling, has contributed to the increased demand for scoop stretchers.</p><p>In addition, the advancements in technology have led to the development of lightweight and portable scoop stretchers, which have further fueled the market growth. These innovative stretchers reduce the discomfort and risk of further injury during patient transportation.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is also witnessing a surge in demand due to the rising adoption of scoop stretchers in military and defense applications.
