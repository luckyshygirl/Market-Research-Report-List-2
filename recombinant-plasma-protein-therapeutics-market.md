Recombinant plasma protein therapeutics help replace these missing clotting factors in patients, reducing the risk of bleeding and promoting blood coagulation. Von Willebrand Disease is a disorder characterized by a deficiency or malfunction of von Willebrand factor, a protein crucial for blood clotting. Recombinant plasma protein therapeutics assist in managing the symptoms and reducing bleeding in patients with this condition.</p></p>
