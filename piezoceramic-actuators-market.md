<p><p>Piezoceramic actuators are devices that convert electrical energy into mechanical motion. Multilayer actuators consist of multiple layers of piezoceramic materials stacked together, providing high displacement with low voltage. Stacked actuators are similar but have larger displacement and higher voltage requirements. Shear actuators generate motion by applying shear stress to the piezoceramic material. Amplified actuators use mechanical amplifiers to increase the displacement of the piezoceramic material. These different types cater to various applications and offer flexibility in terms of displacement, voltage, and motion generation.</p></p>
