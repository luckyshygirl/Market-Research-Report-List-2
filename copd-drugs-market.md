<p><p>The COPD drugs market is categorized into various types. Bronchodilators are medications that relax the airway muscles, allowing for easier breathing. PDE-4 inhibitors work by reducing lung inflammation and improving airflow. Steroids help to reduce inflammation in the airways. Combination therapies involve using multiple medications to control symptoms and improve lung function. Other types of COPD drugs include mucolytics, which help to thin mucus in the lungs, and antibiotics, which are used to treat respiratory infections. Overall, these different types of drugs aim to alleviate symptoms and improve the quality of life for individuals with COPD.</p></p>
