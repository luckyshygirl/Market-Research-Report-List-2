<p><strong>The Refrigerant Gas Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) Sensors</li><li>Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Refrigerant Gas Sensor Module market includes two main types of sensors: NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) Sensors and Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensors. </p><p>NDIR Sensors use infrared light to detect specific gases by measuring the absorption of infrared radiation. These sensors are known for their accuracy, stability, and resistance to interference. </p><p>Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensors work by measuring the change in electrical resistance in a metal oxide film when it comes into contact with certain gases. These sensors are cost-effective, versatile, and easy to manufacture. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in detecting refrigerant gases in various industries.</p></p>
