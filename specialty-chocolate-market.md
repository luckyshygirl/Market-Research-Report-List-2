<p><p>Specialty chocolate comes in various types, such as white and black chocolate. White chocolate is made from cocoa butter, sugar, and milk solids and has a creamy and sweet flavor. It lacks cocoa solids, which gives it its white color. On the other hand, black chocolate, also known as dark chocolate, has a higher percentage of cocoa solids, resulting in a rich and intense flavor. It may contain less sugar and sometimes has a bitter taste. Both types cater to different preferences and offer unique taste experiences in the specialty chocolate market.</p></p>
