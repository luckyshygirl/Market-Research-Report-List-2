<p><p>The portable near infrared (NIR) analyzers market consists of three main types: FT-NIR, AOTF-NIR, and Other. FT-NIR (Fourier-transform NIR) analyzers employ a spectrometer to measure light absorbance, providing high accuracy and versatility for various applications. AOTF-NIR (Acousto-optic tunable filter NIR) analyzers use crystal technology to filter light, enabling rapid measurements. Lastly, the Other category includes different types of portable NIR analyzers that may utilize unique techniques or technologies. These portable analyzers are widely used in industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and environmental monitoring for quick and reliable analysis.</p></p>
