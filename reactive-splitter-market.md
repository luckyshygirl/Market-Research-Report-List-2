<p><p>Reactive splitters are devices used to split an incoming signal into multiple output signals. There are several types of reactive splitters available in the market, including 2-way, 3-way, and 4-way reactive splitters. </p><p>A 2-way reactive splitter splits the signal into two equal parts, sending half of the input signal power to each output. Similarly, a 3-way reactive splitter divides the signal into three equal parts, while a 4-way reactive splitter splits the signal into four equal parts. These splitters are commonly used in applications such as telecommunications, networking, and audio/video distribution systems.</p></p>
