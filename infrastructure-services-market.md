<p><p>Infrastructure Services Market Types:</p><p>1. Public Cloud Market: The public cloud market refers to infrastructure services offered by third-party providers on a shared platform accessible to multiple users over the internet. It involves the hosting, storage, and computing resources that are owned and managed by the service provider. Public cloud services are cost-effective and scalable, making them suitable for businesses with varying demands.</p><p>2. Private Cloud Market: The private cloud market offers infrastructure services that are exclusively dedicated to a single organization. These services can be hosted on-premises or by a third-party provider. Private clouds offer enhanced security, control, and customization options, making them suitable for businesses with strict data privacy regulations and specific requirements.</p><p>3. Hybrid Cloud Market: The hybrid cloud market combines both public and private cloud infrastructure services. It involves the integration of these two environments, allowing organizations to leverage the benefits of both models. Hybrid clouds offer flexibility, scalability, and cost optimization by enabling companies to maintain sensitive data in a private cloud while taking advantage of the public cloud for non-sensitive workloads or peak demands.</p></p>
