<p><p>The Piano DIP Switches market consists of two types of mounting options: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. In surface-mounting, the switches are directly soldered onto the surface of the printed circuit board (PCB), offering a compact and space-saving solution. On the other hand, in through-hole mounting, the switches have leads attached, allowing them to be inserted into holes on the PCB and then soldered on the opposite side for a more secure and sturdy connection. Both types cater to different installation needs, providing options for various design requirements in the Piano DIP Switches market.</p></p>
