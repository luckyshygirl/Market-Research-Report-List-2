<p><strong>The Air Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Filters</li><li>Sub-HEPA Filters</li><li>Secondary Filters</li><li>HEPA and ULPA Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air filters market comprises various types of filters, starting with pre-filters, which remove larger particles and debris from the air. Sub-HEPA filters come next, targeting smaller particles that pre-filters may have missed. Secondary filters further improve air quality by capturing finer pollutants. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters remove almost all airborne particles, whereas ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters offer the highest filtration efficiency, eliminating even tinier contaminants. These different filters cater to diverse needs, ensuring cleaner and healthier indoor air quality.</p></p>
