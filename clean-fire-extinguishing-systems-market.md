<p><p>The Clean Fire Extinguishing Systems market is categorized into three types: Inert Gases, Perfluorohexanone, and Other. Inert Gases use a mixture of nitrogen and argon, which reduces the oxygen levels in the fire to suppress it. Perfluorohexanone, also known as Novec 1230, is a clean agent that removes heat from the fire through its high heat absorption capacity. The Other category includes alternative clean agents such as carbon dioxide, foam, and water mist. These systems are designed to minimize damage to the environment and human health while effectively extinguishing fires.</p></p>
