<p><p>Hydraulic adapters refer to connectors used to join different hydraulic components in a system. There are three main types of hydraulic adapters in the market. O-ring adapters use an O-ring to form a seal between the adapter and the component, ensuring a leak-free connection. Mated angle adapters have a specific angled surface that mates with a corresponding surface on the component, providing a secure fit. Threaded adapters utilize threaded connections to create a tight seal. These different types cater to varying needs and applications in the hydraulic industry.</p></p>
