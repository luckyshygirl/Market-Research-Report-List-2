The rise in consumer electronics, such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops, has boosted the need for reliable and durable enclosures to protect these devices. Additionally, the growing automotive industry and the adoption of advanced technologies like electric vehicles drive the demand for aluminium enclosures to house various electrical components.</p><p>Furthermore, the trend of miniaturization and lightweight designs in the electronics industry has increased the demand for small and compact enclosures made from aluminium.
