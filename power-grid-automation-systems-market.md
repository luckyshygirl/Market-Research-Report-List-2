<p><p>Power Grid Automation Systems refer to technologies that automate the control, monitoring, and management of power grids. On-Grid Automation Systems are used in interconnected power grids, where electricity is generated centrally and distributed to consumers. They enable efficient power flow, fault identification, and grid stability. Off-Grid Automation Systems, on the other hand, are employed in isolated or remote areas where power generation is self-reliant, often through renewable sources. They ensure reliable electricity supply, optimize energy usage, and may include smart microgrids for improved management.</p></p>
