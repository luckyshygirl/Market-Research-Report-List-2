<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The liquid applied membrane market consists of three main types: bituminous, elastomeric, and cementitious membranes. Bituminous membranes are made from asphalt and provide excellent waterproofing capabilities. Elastomeric membranes are made from synthetic rubber compounds and offer high flexibility and durability, making them ideal for varying weather conditions. Cementitious membranes are made from a mixture of cement, polymers, and additives, providing a strong and durable protective layer. All three types are liquid-applied, allowing for easy installation and seamless coverage, enhancing their performance and longevity.</p></p>
