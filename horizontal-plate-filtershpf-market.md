<p><p>Horizontal Plate Filters (HPF) are types of industrial filters used for the separation of solids from liquids. There are two market types of HPF systems. The first is the Reverse Flow Horizontal Plate Filter, which operates by sending the liquid to be filtered in the opposite direction of the flow through the filter plates. The second type is the Standard Horizontal Plate Filter, where the liquid flows through the filter plates in the same direction as the flow. These filters are widely used in various industries for efficient solid-liquid separation processes.</p></p>
