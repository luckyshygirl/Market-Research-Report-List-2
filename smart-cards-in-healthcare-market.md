<p><p>In the healthcare market, contact-based smart cards are a type of technology that requires physical contact with a card reader to transmit information. Contactless smart cards, on the other hand, use wireless technology to transmit data without the need for physical contact. Dual-interface smart cards combine both contact-based and contactless technologies, allowing for versatile usage. Meanwhile, hybrid smart cards integrate multiple technologies, such as magnetic stripes or barcodes, along with the smart card technology. These various types of smart cards cater to different needs and preferences within the healthcare industry.</p></p>
