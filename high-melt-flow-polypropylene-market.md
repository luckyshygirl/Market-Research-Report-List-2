<p><ul><li>Homopolymer</li><li>Copolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Melt Flow Polypropylene market can be categorized into two types - Homopolymer and Copolymer. Homopolymer refers to a type of polypropylene that is made up of only propylene molecules, resulting in a high melting point and stiffness. On the other hand, Copolymer is a blend of propylene and ethylene molecules, offering improved impact resistance and flexibility compared to Homopolymer. Both types find applications in various industries and play a significant role in the overall High Melt Flow Polypropylene market.</p></p>
