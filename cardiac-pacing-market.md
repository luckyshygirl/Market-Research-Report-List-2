<p><strong>The Cardiac Pacing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temporary Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Single-Chamber Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Dual-Chamber Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Single-Chamber ICD</li><li>Dual-Chamber ICD</li><li>Three- Chamber CRT-P</li><li>Three- Chamber CRT-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac pacing market encompasses various types of devices used in the treatment of heart rhythm disorders. Temporary cardiac pacing involves the use of external devices for short-term pacing needs. Single-chamber and dual-chamber cardiac pacing devices are implanted in the heart and provide pacing to either one or two chambers, respectively. Single-chamber and dual-chamber implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs) are also used for both pacing and delivering electrical shocks to treat life-threatening arrhythmias. Three-chamber cardiac resynchronization therapy (CRT) devices, including CRT-P and CRT-D, are used in patients with heart failure to synchronize the pumping of all three heart chambers and improve overall heart function.</p></p>

In heart failure patients, pacing improves heart function, while in individuals with syncope, it helps prevent sudden loss of consciousness.
