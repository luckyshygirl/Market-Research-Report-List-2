<p><ul><li>Special Processes</li><li>Common Craft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCB Assembly market types can be broadly divided into two categories: special processes and common craft. Special processes refer to the production of PCB assemblies that require advanced and specialized techniques, such as surface mount technology (SMT), ball grid array (BGA), and through-hole assembly. These assemblies are usually used in high-end electronic devices and industries that demand precise and complex electronic components. On the other hand, common craft market pertains to the production of PCB assemblies using standard techniques and components, catering to the general electronic industry, consumer electronics, and low to medium-end applications.</p></p>
