<p><p>Air deodorizer and sterilizer are products used to refresh and clean the air in enclosed spaces. Rising concerns regarding respiratory diseases, allergies, and infections have led to the adoption of these products in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. The COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the demand for air deodorizers and sterilizers as people prioritize maintaining clean and safe indoor environments.</p><p>The market is also driven by advancements in technology, such as the development of smart air purifiers that can be controlled remotely through smartphone applications.
