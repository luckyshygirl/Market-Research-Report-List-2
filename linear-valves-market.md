<p><p>Linear valves are used to control the flow of liquids or gases in a linear motion. Gate valves are a common type of linear valve that uses a sliding gate to regulate the flow. Diaphragm valves use a flexible diaphragm to control the flow by physically blocking it. Other types of linear valves include globe valves, needle valves, and pinch valves, which are utilized for specific applications. These valves are an essential component in various industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing, ensuring efficient and precise fluid control.</p></p>
