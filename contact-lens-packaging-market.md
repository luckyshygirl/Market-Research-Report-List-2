<p><p>Contact lens packaging market consists of two major types: disposable and reusable. Disposable contact lens packaging refers to the packaging designed for single-use lenses that are discarded after a specific duration, typically ranging from one day to one month. These types of lenses are more convenient and hygienic, and the packaging is often compact and easy to open. Reusable contact lens packaging, on the other hand, is designed for lenses that can be reused for a longer time with proper cleaning and maintenance. The packaging is usually more durable and provides a secure storage solution for the lenses.</p></p>
