<p><strong>The Flexible AC Transmission System (FACTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Compensation</li><li>Series Compensation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission System (FACTS) is a technology used to regulate and control power flow in an AC transmission system. Shunt Compensation is a type of FACTS that improves the voltage profile and stability of the transmission system by injecting or absorbing reactive power. It includes devices like Static Var Compensators (SVC) and Static Synchronous Compensators (STATCOM). Series Compensation, on the other hand, improves the transmission system's power transfer capability by adjusting line impedance using devices like Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitors (TCSC) and Fixed Series Capacitors (FSC). Both play critical roles in enhancing power transmission efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
