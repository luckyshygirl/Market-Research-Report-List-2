Cloud-based simulation software offers several benefits, including flexibility, scalability, and easy collaboration among multiple users or teams. <p><p>The factory simulation software market can be classified into two types: 2D and 3D. 2D simulation software represents the objects in a two-dimensional plane with only length and width dimensions, lacking depth perception. It is suitable for modeling simple processes and layouts. On the other hand, 3D simulation software represents objects in a three-dimensional space, providing a more realistic and immersive experience. It enables the modeling of complex factory layouts, detailed machinery, and movements, allowing users to visualize and analyze processes more accurately.</p></p>
