<p><p>HDTV Antennas are available in two main market types, namely indoor and outdoor antennas. Indoor antennas are designed to be placed inside a building near a television set. They are typically compact and don't require mounting or external installation. On the other hand, outdoor antennas are designed to be installed outside the building, usually on the rooftop or in the attic. They are larger in size and often offer a higher signal range and quality due to better positioning. The choice between indoor and outdoor antennas depends on the user's location, signal strength, and personal preferences.</p></p>
