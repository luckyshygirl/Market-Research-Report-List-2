<p><p>Fastening accessories are components used to secure, connect, or fasten objects together. They are commonly used in industries such as construction, automotive, aerospace, and furniture manufacturing.</p><p>The fastening accessories market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand from various end-use industries. The growth in the construction industry, especially in emerging economies, is a major factor driving market growth. With rapid urbanization and infrastructure development, the construction industry is witnessing a surge in demand for fastening accessories. Additionally, the automotive industry is also contributing to the market growth as vehicles require a large number of fastening accessories for assembly and repair purposes.</p><p>Technological advancements in fastening accessories are also fueling market growth. Manufacturers are developing innovative products to enhance the efficiency and durability of fastening solutions. For instance, self-tapping screws that eliminate the need for pre-drilling or thread-tapping have gained significant popularity in recent years.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a shift towards eco-friendly fastening accessories. This trend is primarily driven by increasing environmental concerns and stringent regulations regarding waste management and sustainability. Manufacturers are focusing on developing fastening accessories made from recyclable or biodegradable materials.</p><p>The advent of e-commerce platforms has also positively impacted the market growth. Online channels provide easy access to a wide range of fastening accessories, allowing customers to compare prices and choose products as per their requirements.</p><p>Overall, the global fastening accessories market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.
