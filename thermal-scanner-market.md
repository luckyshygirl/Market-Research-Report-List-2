<p><p>Thermal scanners are devices used to measure temperature by capturing infrared radiation emitted by objects. The market for thermal scanners can be divided into two types: fixed and portable. Fixed thermal scanners are designed to be permanently installed in a specific location, such as entrances of buildings or manufacturing facilities, to monitor body temperature and identify potential fevers in a non-invasive way. Portable thermal scanners, on the other hand, are smaller and more mobile devices that can be easily carried and used on-the-go, making them suitable for applications such as spot-checking temperatures in public places or during travel.</p></p>
