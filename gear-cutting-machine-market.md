<p><p>The gear cutting machine market is divided into various types, including gear hobbing machines, gear shaping machines, gear shaving machines, and gear grinding machines. Gear hobbing machines are used to cut teeth into gears by generating continuous motion. Gear shaping machines use a cutting tool to gradually shape the teeth of the gear. Gear shaving machines remove a small amount of material from the gear's surface to improve its finish and accuracy. Gear grinding machines employ abrasive wheels to produce gears with precise dimensions and smooth surfaces.</p></p>
