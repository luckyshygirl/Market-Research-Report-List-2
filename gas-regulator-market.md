<p><p>The gas regulator market consists of various types of regulators designed to control the pressure of gas flow in different applications. Single stage pressure reducing regulators are used to reduce high-pressure gas to a lower, more manageable pressure. Back pressure regulators help maintain constant pressure upstream of a system. Two-stage pressure reducing regulators provide more accurate pressure control by using two sets of valves. Single stage gas cylinder regulators regulate the pressure of gas in small gas cylinders. Two-stage gas cylinder regulators are similar but offer increased pressure control accuracy. Other types of regulators include high purity gas regulators and specialty gas regulators.</p></p>
