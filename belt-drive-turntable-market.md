<p><p>The belt-drive turntable market comprises three types of cartridges: moving-magnetic (MM), moving-iron (MI), and moving-coil (MC). A moving-magnetic cartridge utilizes a magnet to generate an electrical signal, providing a balanced audio performance. Moving-iron cartridges employ a piece of iron that moves between two magnets to produce sound, offering a rich and well-defined sound quality. Moving-coil cartridges possess a coil that moves within a magnetic field, delivering stellar clarity and responsiveness in audio reproduction. These different types cater to varying preferences and aim to enhance the overall listening experience.</p></p>
