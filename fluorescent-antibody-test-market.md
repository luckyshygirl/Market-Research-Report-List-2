<p><strong>The Fluorescent Antibody Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Test</li><li>Indirect Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fluorescent Antibody Test (FAT) is a diagnostic tool used to identify and detect specific antigens or antibodies in a patient's sample. The Direct Test market involves using fluorescent-labeled antibodies to directly target and bind to the antigen or virus under examination. This method provides quick results by directly amplifying the fluorescence signal. In contrast, the Indirect Test market involves two-step testing, where primary antibodies bind to the antigen, and secondary fluorescent-labeled antibodies bind to the primary antibodies, resulting in amplified fluorescence signals. This approach allows for increased sensitivity and flexibility in detecting various antigens.</p></p>
