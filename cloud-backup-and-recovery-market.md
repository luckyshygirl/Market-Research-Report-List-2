<p><p>Cloud backup and recovery market includes three types: private cloud, public cloud, and hybrid cloud. Private cloud refers to an exclusive network and data storage facility owned and operated by a single organization, providing enhanced security and control. On the other hand, public cloud offers services to multiple organizations over a shared infrastructure managed by a third-party provider, and it is more cost-effective. Lastly, hybrid cloud combines both private and public clouds, allowing organizations to enjoy the benefits of both in terms of flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency.</p></p>
