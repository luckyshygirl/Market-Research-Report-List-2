<p><p>The automatic hair braider market offers different types of braids, including the three strand braid and the four strand braid. The three strand braid, also known as a simple braid, is the most commonly used technique where three sections of hair are woven together in a simple crisscross pattern. On the other hand, the four strand braid, also called a fishtail braid, involves dividing the hair into four sections and intricately weaving them together to create a stylish and trendy braided look. These market offerings provide users with options to achieve different hairstyles efficiently.</p></p>
