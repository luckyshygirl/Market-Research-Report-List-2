<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) Pad Conditioners market consists of two main types: Conventional Pad Conditioners and CVD Diamond Pad Conditioners. Conventional Pad Conditioners are commonly made of polyurethane or resin materials and are widely used in CMP processes. They provide good pad conditioning performance at a relatively lower cost. On the other hand, CVD Diamond Pad Conditioners are made using diamond coatings deposited on the pad surface through the Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) technique. These conditioners offer superior durability and longer lifespan, resulting in more efficient CMP operations.</p></p>
