<p><strong>The Corrective Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Divergent Lens</li><li>Convergent Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Corrective Lenses market consists of two types: Divergent Lens and Convergent Lens. Divergent Lens is designed to spread out light rays and is used to correct nearsightedness or myopia. It helps focus the light rays falling on the eye's retina. On the other hand, Convergent Lens is used to correct farsightedness or hyperopia, as it brings the light rays together to form a sharp image on the retina. These lenses are widely available in the market and can be prescribed by optometrists to provide vision correction for people with refractive errors.</p></p>
