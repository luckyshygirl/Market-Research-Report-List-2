<p><p>Retractable roof systems are available in three main types: inclined, curved, and flat. Inclined retractable roofs are designed with a sloping angle, allowing for effective water drainage and prevention of snow accumulation. Curved retractable roofs, as the name suggests, feature a curved design, providing a unique aesthetic while maintaining functionality. Flat retractable roofs, on the other hand, are designed with a horizontal surface, ensuring a sleek and minimalistic look. Each type caters to different architectural designs and preferences, offering varying styles and functionality in the retractable roof system market.</p></p>
