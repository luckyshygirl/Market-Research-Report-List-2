<p><p>Metal powder atomization equipment is used to produce metal powders through a process called atomization. In this process, molten metal is converted into fine, spherical particles. There are three main types of metal powder atomization equipment: gas atomization equipment, water atomization equipment, and other market. Gas atomization equipment uses high-pressure gas jets to break up the molten metal stream. Water atomization equipment uses water jets for the same purpose. The other market refers to any alternative or specialized equipment used for metal powder atomization, such as plasma atomization or centrifugal atomization.</p></p>
