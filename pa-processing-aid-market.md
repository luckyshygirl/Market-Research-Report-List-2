<p><p>Processing aids are substances that are added to the polymer manufacturing process to improve its properties and performance. There are two main types of processing aids: Polymer Processing Aid (PPA) and Acrylic Processing Aid (APA) markets. PPA helps in increasing the melt strength, reducing die build-up, and improving the surface finish of polymers. On the other hand, APA is specifically used to enhance the processing and performance of acrylic polymers. Both markets cater to the need for enhanced polymer processing, allowing for improved efficiency and quality in various industries.</p></p>
