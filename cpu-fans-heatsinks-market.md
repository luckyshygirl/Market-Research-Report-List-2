<p><p>The CPU fans and heatsinks market consists of various types, including big air CPU coolers and mid-air CPU coolers. Big air CPU coolers are larger in size and use a combination of heat pipes and large fans to efficiently dissipate heat from the CPU. They are commonly used in gaming or high-performance systems. On the other hand, mid-air CPU coolers are smaller and use a single fan to cool the CPU.
