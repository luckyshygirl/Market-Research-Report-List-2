<p><p>The veterinary coagulation analyzer market includes three types: automated, semi-automated, and manual. The automated analyzers are advanced machines that can perform coagulation tests with minimal human intervention. They offer high accuracy, precision, and quick results, making them suitable for large-volume testing. Semi-automated analyzers require some human involvement for sample preparation and result interpretation. They are less expensive than automated analyzers and are preferred for medium-volume testing. Manual analyzers are basic instruments that rely entirely on human effort for sample preparation, reagent addition, and result interpretation, making them suitable for low-volume testing.</p></p>
