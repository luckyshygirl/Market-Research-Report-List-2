<p><p>The electrical substation market can be categorized into three types: AIS substation, GIS substation, and Others. AIS substation refers to Air Insulated Substation, where the equipment is open-air and not enclosed in a building. GIS substation refers to Gas Insulated Substation, where the equipment is enclosed in a metal housing with insulating gas. The Others category includes various substation types that do not fall into the previous two categories. These different substation types cater to varying needs and requirements of the power industry.</p></p>
