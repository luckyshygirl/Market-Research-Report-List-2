<p><p>In the personal care market, UVA filters are used to protect the skin and hair from harmful UVA rays. Water-phase UVA filters are water-soluble ingredients that work by absorbing or scattering UVA radiation. They are commonly used in products like lotions, creams, and serums that have a high water content. Oil-phase UVA filters, on the other hand, are oil-soluble ingredients that work by creating a barrier on the skin or hair to block UVA rays. These filters are typically used in oil-based products such as sunscreens, facial oils, and hair oils.</p></p>
