<p><p>A Vector Network Analyzer (VNA) is an electronic test equipment used to measure the complex transmission and reflection characteristics of devices and components in high-frequency networks. It operates by injecting a known signal into the system under test and measuring the response to determine important parameters such as impedance, scattering parameters, and insertion loss. VNAs are extensively used in various industries including telecommunications, aerospace and defense, electronics, and automotive.</p><p>The current outlook of the Vector Network Analyzer market is highly positive.
