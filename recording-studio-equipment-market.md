Microphones capture sound, while headphones and studio monitors provide accurate sound reproduction. Cables transmit audio signals between devices, while microphone stands hold the microphones in place. Pop filters minimize plosive sounds during vocal recording. Each of these components plays a vital role in creating professional recordings in the music industry.</p></p>
