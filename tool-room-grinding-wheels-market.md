The market for these wheels can be categorized into different types, namely straight wheel, tapered wheel, straight cup, and dish cup. </p><p>Straight wheels are used for cylindrical, surface, and tool grinding. Tapered wheels are specifically designed for tool and cutter grinding. Straight cup wheels have a slightly concave shape and are used for surface and cylindrical grinding. Dish cup wheels have a shallow dish-like shape and are used for tool and cutter sharpening. These varying types of grinding wheels cater to different grinding applications and requirements in the tool room industry.</p></p>
