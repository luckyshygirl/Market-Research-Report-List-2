<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Method</li><li>Microplate Method</li><li>Oxidation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Creatinine detection kits market types include colorimetric method, microplate method, and oxidation method. </p><p>Colorimetric method involves the measurement of creatinine levels by comparing the intensity of color produced. </p><p>Microplate method uses specialized plates to measure the concentration of creatinine in a sample by the reaction of creatinine with specific reagents. </p><p>Oxidation method involves the conversion of creatinine into a colored product through a series of chemical reactions, which is then measured to determine the creatinine concentration. These methods are commonly used in laboratories for the detection and quantification of creatinine levels in biological samples.</p></p>
