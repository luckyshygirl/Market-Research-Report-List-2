<p><p>The Automatic Boom Barrier market is segmented into three types: Remote Control, RFID Tags/Reader, and Loop Detectors. Remote Control barriers are operated through remote devices, offering convenient access control. RFID Tags/Reader barriers use radio frequency identification technology to grant access to authorized individuals through RFID tags. Loop Detectors barriers utilize underground loops to detect vehicles and open the barrier accordingly. These market types cater to different preferences and requirements, providing various options for efficient and secure automated access control.</p></p>
