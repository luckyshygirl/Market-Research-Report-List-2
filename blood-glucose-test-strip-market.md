<p><p>Blood glucose test strips are used in the medical field to measure an individual's blood sugar levels. There are two main types of test strips available in the market: Glucose Oxidase and Glucose Dehydrogenase. Glucose Oxidase test strips work by converting glucose into a compound that produces an electric current, providing a reading of blood sugar levels. On the other hand, Glucose Dehydrogenase test strips use a different enzyme to break down glucose and produce an electrical signal to measure blood glucose levels. Both types aid in monitoring and managing diabetes.</p></p>
