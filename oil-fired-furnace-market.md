<p><p>The oil-fired furnace market comprises three main types: Internal Condensing Oil Fired Furnace, External Condensing Oil Fired Furnace, and Non-Condensing Oil Fired Furnace. Internal condensing furnaces are designed to capture and utilize the heat from the flue gases, improving energy efficiency. External condensing furnaces typically have separate combustion and heat exchanger chambers, making them more compact and easier to install. Non-condensing furnaces do not incorporate condensing technology and therefore have lower energy efficiency. Each type caters to different customer preferences and energy-saving requirements.</p></p>
