<p><p>Multicore cables are used to transmit multiple signals or power through a single cable. Solid cables have a rigid core and are suitable for permanent installations. Standard cables are commonly used in various applications and offer flexibility. Flexible cables are designed to withstand bending and movements, making them ideal for moving parts or mobile devices. Flat cables have a flat shape and are often used in tight spaces or for cable management. Parallel twin cables consist of two cores side by side and are commonly used for data transmission in telecommunications and computer networks.</p></p>
