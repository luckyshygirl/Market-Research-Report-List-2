<p><p>The Automatic Identification System (AIS) transponder market offers two major types: Class A Marine AIS and Class B Marine AIS. Class A AIS is mandatory for larger vessels and offers high transmission power, providing accurate and real-time vessel data. It ensures efficient collision avoidance and seamless integration with other navigation systems. On the other hand, Class B AIS is voluntary and primarily used by smaller vessels. It has reduced transmission power but still enables vessel identification, navigation, and safety information transmission, aiding in situational awareness and preventing accidents.</p></p>
