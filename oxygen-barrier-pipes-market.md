The oxygen barrier layer within the pipe wall prevents the corrosion of metallic components in the system, thereby increasing its lifespan and reliability.</p><p>The future outlook for the oxygen barrier pipes market looks promising. PE-RT Oxygen Barrier Pipes are made from a type of polyethylene and are known for their flexibility and resistance to high temperatures. PEX Oxygen Barrier Pipes are made from cross-linked polyethylene and are highly resistant to corrosion. PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes are made from a type of polybutylene and are durable and easy to install. These types of pipes are designed to prevent the infiltration of oxygen into the water supply.</p></p>
