<p><p>The public cloud non-relational databases, also known as NoSQL databases, consist of different market types. Column Storage Databases organize data in columns, enabling efficient handling of large datasets and analytics. Document Databases store, retrieve, and manage semi-structured data, like JSON, in document format. Graph Databases focus on relationships between data entities, suitable for complex interconnected data models. Each market type offers distinct benefits and is designed to address specific data storage and retrieval requirements.</p></p>
