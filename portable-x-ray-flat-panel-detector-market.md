<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Portable X-Ray Flat Panel Detector market comprises two main types: Direct Conversion and Indirect Conversion. Direct Conversion detectors use a material that directly converts X-ray photons into electrical signals, which are then converted into digital images. This type offers high image quality and sensitivity. On the other hand, Indirect Conversion detectors use a material that converts X-ray photons into visible light, which is then converted into electrical signals and transformed into digital images. Although indirect conversion detectors are less sensitive than direct conversion ones, they are more cost-effective.</p></p>
