<p><p>PTFE Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are used in various electronic devices and have two main market types: Single Layer and Multi-layer. Single layer PCBs consist of a single layer of PTFE material with a conductive layer on one side, making them suitable for simpler electronic applications. On the other hand, Multi-layer PCBs have multiple layers of PTFE material and conductive layers, allowing for more complex circuit designs and higher functionality. These PCBs are commonly used in advanced electronic devices requiring intricate circuitry, such as computers, smartphones, and aerospace equipment.</p></p>
