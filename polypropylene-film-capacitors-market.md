<p><p>Polypropylene film capacitors are widely used in various electronic applications. There are two main types of polypropylene film capacitors in the market: plain and metalized. Plain polypropylene film capacitors are constructed with two plain polypropylene film layers, while metalized polypropylene film capacitors have one metallized film layer. Metalized capacitors offer better self-healing properties and are used for applications that require high stability. On the other hand, plain capacitors are cost-effective and commonly used in low to medium-voltage applications. Both types of capacitors provide high-capacity performance and are essential components in electrical circuits.</p></p>
