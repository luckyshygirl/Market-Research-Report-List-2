<p><p>Variable displacement pumps are widely used in various industries for fluid transfer and control. The market for variable displacement pumps is categorized into two types: variable displacement piston pumps and variable displacement vane pumps. Variable displacement piston pumps use pistons to generate high pressure, providing consistent flow rate even under varying operating conditions. On the other hand, variable displacement vane pumps use rotating vanes to create suction and generate pressure, ensuring efficient fluid transfer. Both types offer advantages such as flexibility, improved energy efficiency, and enhanced performance in different applications.</p></p>
