<p><strong>The UV/Visible Spectroscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-beam Systems</li><li>Dual-beam Systems</li><li>Array-based Systems</li><li>Handheld Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV/Visible spectroscopy market includes various types of systems. Single-beam systems use a single light beam to measure the absorption of a sample, while dual-beam systems split the light beam into two paths, one passing through the sample and the other through a reference. Array-based systems use a linear array of detectors to measure multiple wavelengths simultaneously. Handheld systems are portable devices that allow on-site measurements. These different types cater to various applications and offer different capabilities, providing flexibility and convenience to users in the UV/Visible spectroscopy market.</p></p>
