<p><p>The spine fixation market consists of various types of devices used to stabilize and support the spine during surgical procedures. Some common types include rods, plates, screws, and others. Rods are long metallic structures implanted along the spine to provide stability. Plates are flat metal plates placed on the surface of the spine to hold it in place. Screws are threaded devices used to fix the rods or plates to the vertebrae. Other devices may include hooks, cages, or interbody devices that assist in spinal fusion procedures. These devices are critical in treating spinal conditions like fractures, deformities, or degenerative diseases.</p></p>
