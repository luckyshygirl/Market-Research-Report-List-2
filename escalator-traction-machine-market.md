<p><p>The Escalator Traction Machine market can be categorized into two types: Gearless Traction Machine and Geared Traction Machine.  </p><p>Gearless Traction Machines utilize a direct drive system, eliminating the need for gears. This results in a more efficient and compact design, offering higher energy savings and a smoother ride experience for escalator users. </p><p>On the other hand, Geared Traction Machines rely on gear systems to provide power to the escalator. While they may not be as energy-efficient as gearless machines, they are generally more cost-effective and suitable for smaller-scale installations.</p></p>
