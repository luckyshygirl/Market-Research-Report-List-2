<p><p>The cool chain, also known as the cold chain, refers to the process of transporting and storing products under specific temperature conditions to ensure their quality and safety. Increasing demand for temperature- sensitive products, especially in the pharmaceutical and food industries, is the key driver of this market. Pharmaceuticals need to be stored and transported under precise temperature conditions to maintain their effectiveness, which has led to a rise in the adoption of cool chain logistics solutions. Similarly, the growth of the food industry and the need for safe and fresh products have fueled the demand for cool chain services.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have had a positive impact on the cool chain market.
