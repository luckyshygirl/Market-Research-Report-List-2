<p><p>Metal growlers are containers used to store and transport craft beer. They are typically made of stainless steel and come with a screw-on cap to keep the beverage fresh and carbonated. Metal growlers offer a premium alternative to traditional glass growlers, as they are less prone to breakage and offer better insulation.</p><p>Additionally, the metal growlers market has witnessed various trends in recent years. One such trend is the customization of metal growlers to cater to individual preferences and branding. These features help ensure that the beverage remains fresh and flavorful, even after extended periods of storage.</p><p>Overall, the metal growlers market is poised for significant growth in the coming years, driven by the increased demand for sustainable packaging solutions, the rising popularity of craft beer, and the introduction of innovative features in metal growler designs.</p></p>
