<p><ul><li>Electrochemical</li><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Infrared</li><li>Photo ionization detector (PID)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial gas sensors market consists of four main types: electrochemical, semiconductor, infrared, and photo ionization detector (PID). Electrochemical sensors use a chemical reaction to detect and measure gas concentrations, providing accurate and reliable results. Semiconductor sensors employ a microchip to detect gases and are widely used due to their low cost and small size. Infrared sensors determine gas concentrations by measuring the absorption of infrared light. PID sensors use ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules and detect their concentrations. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
