<p><p>In the steel cord for radial tires market, there are four main types based on tensile strength levels: normal tensile (NT), high tensile (HT), super tensile (ST), and ultra tensile (UT). Normal tensile steel cords offer standard strength for general tire applications. High tensile steel cords have increased strength and are used in heavy-duty tire manufacturing. Super tensile steel cords provide even greater strength for specialized and high-performance tire requirements. Ultra tensile steel cords have the highest tensile strength and are employed in extreme conditions and demanding tire applications.</p></p>
