<p><p>Desalting columns are utilized for removing salt ions from biological samples during research or experiments. There are two main types available in the market: spin desalting columns and gravity desalting columns. Spin desalting columns are designed for spinning applications, where samples are placed in a column and centrifuged to separate the salt ions. On the other hand, gravity desalting columns work by allowing the sample to flow through the column via gravity, effectively removing salt impurities. Both types are widely used in various scientific and biochemical applications.</p></p>
