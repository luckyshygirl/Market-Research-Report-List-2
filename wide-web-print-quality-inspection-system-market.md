<p><ul><li>In-line Print Quality Inspection System</li><li>Off-line Print Quality Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wide Web Print Quality Inspection System market can be categorized into two types: </p><p>1. In-line Print Quality Inspection System: This type of system is integrated within the printing process and performs real-time inspection of print quality while the printing is in progress. It ensures that any defects or errors in the print are promptly detected and corrected, resulting in improved print quality and reduced wastage.</p><p>2. Off-line Print Quality Inspection System: In contrast, off-line systems inspect print quality after the printing process is completed. These systems evaluate the printed materials for any defects or inconsistencies, allowing for timely identification and rectification of issues. Off-line systems are generally used for batch inspection or as a final quality check before the printed materials are released for further processing or distribution.</p></p>
