<p><ul><li>Drainable Ostomy Pouches</li><li>Closed Ostomy Pouches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable ostomy pouches are commonly used by individuals with a temporary or permanent stoma. There are two main types of disposable ostomy pouches: drainable ostomy pouches and closed ostomy pouches. </p><p>Drainable ostomy pouches have an opening at the bottom that allows the contents to be easily emptied into a toilet. This type of pouch is suitable for those with a colostomy or ileostomy who have liquid or semi-liquid output.</p><p>Closed ostomy pouches, on the other hand, are sealed at the bottom and are designed for one-time use. They are suitable for individuals with a colostomy who have more predictable bowel movements and do not require frequent emptying.</p><p>Both types of pouches are available in the market to cater to the varying needs of ostomy patients.</p></p>
