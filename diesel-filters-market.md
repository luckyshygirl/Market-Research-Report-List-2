<p><p>Diesel filters are essential components in vehicles that remove impurities from the fuel to ensure efficient engine performance. There are two main types of diesel filters available in the market: in-line type and built-in type. In-line type filters are installed externally in the fuel line and can be easily replaced. On the other hand, built-in type filters are integrated within the vehicle's fuel system and require professional maintenance for replacement. Both types serve the same purpose of removing contaminants, but differ in terms of installation and maintenance procedures.</p></p>
