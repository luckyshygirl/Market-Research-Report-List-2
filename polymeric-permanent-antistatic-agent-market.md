These agents are incorporated into materials during the manufacturing process to provide long-lasting antistatic properties.</p><p>The market analysis of the polymeric permanent antistatic agent market suggests that it is experiencing steady growth. The increasing demand from various end-use industries, such as electronics and packaging, is driving the market growth. The rapid growth of the electronics industry, coupled with the increasing use of plastics in packaging, is creating a significant demand for polymeric permanent antistatic agents.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing awareness among manufacturers about the benefits of using antistatic agents is also contributing to market growth. These agents provide a safeguard against static discharge, which can cause damage to electronic components or create problems in manufacturing processes.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a rising demand for eco-friendly and sustainable antistatic agents.
