<p><p>Social media monitoring platforms are categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based platforms operate on remote servers, allowing users to access and utilize the software through the internet. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs. On-premises platforms, on the other hand, are installed locally on the user's hardware and managed by their internal IT team. This provides greater control over data security and customization options but requires higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to different organizational needs and preferences in monitoring social media activity.</p></p>
