<p><p>Marine sonar systems are used for underwater mapping and navigation. The single beam scanning sonar system utilizes a single sound wave emitted in a straight line, providing a simple and cost-effective solution. The multi-beam sonar system emits multiple beams simultaneously, yielding wider coverage and higher resolution. The side scan sonar system produces detailed imagery of the seafloor, assisting in detecting objects or obstructions. Other sonar systems include forward-looking and obstacle avoidance sonars, each serving unique purposes in marine operations. These sonar systems cater to diverse needs, offering efficient underwater exploration and safety.</p></p>
