<p><p>Self-inflating lifejackets are personal flotation devices that automatically inflate to provide buoyancy when immersed in water. They are designed to be worn on the body and serve as a safety measure for individuals participating in water-based activities such as boating, fishing, and water sports. These lifejackets consist of an air bladder, which is triggered to inflate upon contact with water, and are typically equipped with reflective materials and whistle for enhanced visibility and signaling.</p><p>The self-inflating lifejackets market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. This can be attributed to several factors such as increasing awareness about water safety, stringent regulations mandating the use of lifejackets, and rising participation in recreational water activities. The growing popularity of water sports and boating activities, coupled with a rise in disposable incomes, is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>In addition, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative self-inflating lifejackets with features such as integrated GPS, personal locator beacons, and backup inflation systems.
