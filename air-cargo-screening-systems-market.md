<p><strong>The Air Cargo Screening Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosive Detection Systems (EDS)</li><li>Explosive Trace Detection (ETD)</li><li>Non-computed Tomography Transmission X-ray (Non-CT X-ray)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Cargo Screening Systems market includes three types of screening technologies. Explosive Detection Systems (EDS) are advanced machines that use computed tomography (CT) technology to detect explosives in cargo. Explosive Trace Detection (ETD) systems analyze residue samples for trace amounts of explosives. Non-computed Tomography Transmission X-ray (Non-CT X-ray) scanners use X-ray technology to examine the contents of cargo for potential threats, without generating a 3D image. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring air cargo safety by detecting explosives and other dangerous materials.</p></p>
