<p><p>The Meat Speciation Testing Service Market consists of various types of tests used to determine the species of meat in food products. These tests include Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and other Molecular Diagnostic Tests such as Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS/MS). PCR identifies the DNA sequences of specific species, ELISA uses antibodies to detect species-specific proteins, and LC-MS/MS analyzes the presence of specific peptides. These tests help in verifying label claims, ensuring food safety, and preventing food fraud.</p></p>
