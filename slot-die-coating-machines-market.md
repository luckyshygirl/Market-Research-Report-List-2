<p><p>Slot-die coating machines are used for efficient and precise coating applications in various industries. There are two types of slot-die coating machines: single-side and two-side market. Single-side machines are designed to coat only one side of the substrate at a time, providing a uniform coating layer on that side. On the other hand, two-side machines are capable of coating both sides of the substrate simultaneously, enhancing productivity and reducing processing time. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application, such as coating thickness, precision, and production speed.</p></p>
