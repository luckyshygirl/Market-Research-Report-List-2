<p><p>The electrosurgical tools market involves three types of technologies: radio frequency, ultrasonic, and molecular resonance. Radio frequency technology uses electrical currents to cut or coagulate tissue during surgery. Ultrasonic technology employs high-frequency sound waves to generate heat for precision cutting or cauterization. Molecular resonance technology uses a combination of radio frequency and magnetic fields to selectively target and destroy cancer cells. These technologies play essential roles in surgical procedures, providing surgeons with versatile tools for cutting, coagulating, and destroying tissue.</p></p>
