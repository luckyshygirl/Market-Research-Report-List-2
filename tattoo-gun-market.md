<p><p>The tattoo gun market comprises three main types: electromagnetic, motor, and pneumatic tattoo guns. Electromagnetic tattoo guns are the most common and use electromagnetic coils to create the needle's up and down movement. Motor tattoo guns utilize a motor to move the needle, resulting in a smoother operation. Pneumatic tattoo guns, on the other hand, work with compressed air, providing artists with increased precision and control. These diverse market types offer tattoo artists different options in terms of functionality and performance to cater to their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
